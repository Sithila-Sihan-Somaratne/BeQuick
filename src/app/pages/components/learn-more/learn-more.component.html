Learn more about BeQuick

You can learn about how to use BeQuick, to sell and rent properties and have details about what you want.


1. Log in or sign up.

To become a member of BeQuick, you first have to be at least 18 years old. If you're a newbie, you have to first sign up by entering your user name (invent it!), email, phone number, location, birth day, and a strong password. After you inserted these credentials, you will receive an email that says to enter a PIN for user verification. After that your PIN is verified, you can finally log in.

2. Start starring ads... or post your own!

Now, after you logged in, you are welcomed in the world of BeQuick! Search for an ad you like with the extra options to choose the right thing you like, you can star it and view it later or start posting your own ad by selecting the options (you can modify them in the future). When posting your ad, an email or a call will come for you to deal with the ad. Also, there's a limit for the ad: one month is the limit for the ad to be shown. If no one is interested, then you should request another month by paying an amount or BeQuick will remove it.

3. Wait for a customer and help.

Last thing to do is to find a customer for your ad. If someone is interested, they will pay the amount you requested through the bank and then you can remove your ad manually. If you need help, go to this link: Contacts Page.
